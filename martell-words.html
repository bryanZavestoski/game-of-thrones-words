House Martell

Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken

The house words of House Martell are ‘Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken,’ arising from the fact that they are the only Great House of Westeros to have never been conquered by outsiders. Much of the Dornish visual style seems to come from the Moorish architecture of Spain and Portugal. Because this style was developed during the Islamic rule of these regions, the lettering is inspired by arabic script.

'Unbowed, unbent, unbroken'. The words of House Martell. A promise to our enemies, and a challenge to our lovers.

-Oberyn Martell
